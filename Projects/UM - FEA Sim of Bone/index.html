Overview

Comprehensive biomechanical analysis using finite element modeling to understand bone behavior under loading conditions for medical device design.

Problem Statement

Medical device designers need to understand how devices interact with bone structures under various loading conditions. Challenge: develop accurate FEA models capturing bone behavior.

Solution & Approach

Performed FEA analysis using CT scan data for geometry, material properties from literature, and realistic loading conditions based on biomechanical research.

Results & Outcomes

Successfully provided design insights enabling optimization of medical devices for better load distribution and reduced failure risk.

Key Learnings

Biomechanical FEA requires accurate material models and realistic boundary conditions. Validation against experimental data is critical for confidence in results.
