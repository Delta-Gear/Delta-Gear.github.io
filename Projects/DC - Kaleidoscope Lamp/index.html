Conceal dynamic lighting effect into seemingly ordinary lamp.

Overview

The Kaleidoscope Lamp is an interactive art installation combining mechanical design, LED lighting, motion control, and computer vision.

Problem Statement

Create an engaging interactive art installation that responds to user presence and movement, blending mechanical precision with creative expression.

Solution & Approach

Designed a rotating mechanical structure with addressable LED strips, motion detection via computer vision, and microcontroller for real-time patterns.

Results & Outcomes

Successfully created an interactive installation that demonstrated the intersection of engineering and creative design.

Key Learnings

Personal projects provide opportunities to explore creative applications of engineering skills. Balance between precision and artistic expression was key.
