Massachusetts Institute of Technology
Applied Data Science Certificate
2023

Overview

Development of machine learning pipelines to process and analyze massive quantities of Google Streetview imagery for urban planning applications.

Problem Statement

Large-scale image analysis requires robust computer vision models handling variations in lighting, perspective, and image quality. Challenge: build scalable models for real-world analysis.

Solution & Approach

Implemented deep learning models for object detection and scene understanding using transfer learning and data augmentation techniques.

Results & Outcomes

Successfully developed models capable of analyzing large-scale imagery and extracting useful insights for urban planning.

Key Learnings

Real-world data revealed the importance of preprocessing, data validation, and understanding domain-specific challenges in computer vision.
