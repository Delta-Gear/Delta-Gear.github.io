Boston Micromachines
Senior Product Engineer
2020 - 2025

Overview

Development of a medical imaging system specialized for retinal examination and diagnostics, requiring integration of complex optical components with electronic control systems.

Problem Statement

Existing retinal imaging systems lacked the precision and ease of use required for modern clinical environments. Challenge: develop a system delivering high-quality images while maintaining ease of operation and reliability.

Solution & Approach

Designed and developed the mechanical and optical architecture for an advanced retinal imaging system. Integration of precision optical components, motorized positioning systems, and electronic controls.

Results & Outcomes

Successfully delivered an imaging system meeting all optical and mechanical specifications with improved imaging quality and reduced setup time.

Key Learnings

This project reinforced the importance of early collaboration between optical, mechanical, and software teams. Medical device development requires particular attention to reliability and user experience.
